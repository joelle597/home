An Introduction to Logic for Computer Science

Course Overview

An Introduction to Logic for Computer Science is a comprehensive course taught by the University of Leeds. This course is designed to introduce students to the logic behind Computer science and how it is applied to derive solutions for problems.

What You'll Learn

  • Propositional logic
  • Modeling with logic
  • Learn to articulate how logic plays an important role in computer science.
  • Apply techniques to derive solutions for modelled problems.

Course Duration

7 hours, with approximately 2-3 hours of study per week
